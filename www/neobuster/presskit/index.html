Description

Introducing Neo Buster!

Neo Buster is an Arcade / Retro / Shoot'em up game with a twist and 2020 visuals which I spent some time building. I would love for you to try it out.

It takes some exciting, proven concepts and blends them to create something new and original.Think R-Type (Shoot'em up) meets Pang (classic bubbles burster) and you get Neo Buster.

Download Neo Buster from https://gamelies.com/neobuster/

Interestingly, the enemies paths and behaviours are not pre-defined; they are gravity driven, and the center of gravity is ... you. As you progress, based on the mass and velocity of the enemies, swarms of bubbles begin chasing you around.

I also wanted to produce a truly challenging game. This is no candy crush ... You will get killed. It is hard and unforgiving. Think Ghosts 'n Goblins difficulty, or more recently Dark Souls. You do have a check-point after each chapter, but to reach that chapter you will need to master each level. So fear not if you need to try over and over.

Features

  • Take your classic R-Type Shoot'em up and mix it with real physics and gravity simulator. You get a new Retro Arcade Experience with fantastic visuals.
  • The amazing techno ambient soundtracks immerses you in a dark atmoshpere.
  • This is not for the faint-hearted. The game really wil become a challenge as difficulty increase sharply. Expect it wil take skill to complete the game.
  • Shoot your way through 8 Chapters, each divided in 7 scenes.
  • Collect power up and Shield up bonuses to help you survive
  • Splitter, exploding, snakes, laser snakes, turret.. only some of the many kind of enemies you will encounter.
