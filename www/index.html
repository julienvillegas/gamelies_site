About me

I am an indie Dev who loves technology and experimenting. The name Gaméliès is derived from Méliès, who was a pioneer in the (then burgeoning) cinematic special effect industry, and spent his life finding different outlets for his creativity. I am no Méliès, but he has been an inspiration to me, so I wanted to include his name in the branding of my gaming projects.
