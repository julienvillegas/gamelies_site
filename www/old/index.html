About US.

Harriet is a passionate teacher who has over 10 years of experience working with children, and promoting creativity, empathy and different ways of telling stories. She has taught Art, Language Arts, ELL, Creative Writing and Health, and is currently a middle school Language Arts teacher at ES International School. She is interested in connecting people through creativity, the interactive classroom, and deep listening. Harriet has trained and coached other teachers, through workshops and conferences such as the MAIS (Mediterranean Association of International Schools) conference in Barcelona 2015. Harriet is also a writer of poetry and prose and has been published in Paginas Rojas, 5 by five writers, Barcelona Ink, Hellenic American Union, and Country Music. She recently finished a book about her experiences volunteering with Amics de la gent gran.

Julien is passionate about building stuff, digitally and in the real world.
